Resource Submission

We welcome the submission of additional resources to complement those on the Employer Engagement Toolkit website. To submit a resource for consideration, please send us a PDF of the resource (or a link to the resource).

To be considered for inclusion on the site, the resource should:

  • Provide evidence-based information to help employers or educators understand the benefits of employer-educator partnerships.
  • Provide guidance in forming and sustaining partnerships.
  • Have been published within the last 5 years.
  • Be available electronically/digitally.
  • Be available at no cost.

If your resource meets these criteria, please email the resource (or a link) to us and we will review it for addition to our site.
