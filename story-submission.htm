Success Story Submission

We welcome the submission of additional stories of success to complement those on the Employer Engagement Toolkit website. To submit an employer story or educator story for consideration, please send us a description of the story's Purpose, About the Program, and Impact (see format of currently posted stories) and a link to the detailed story.

To be considered for inclusion on the site, the story should:

  • Provide insight into the process by which successful partnerships have designed their programs and achieved their goals.
  • Convey a positive message about employer/educator partnerships.
  • Have taken place within the last 5 years.
  • Link to additional information (e.g., web-based article, video, etc.).
  • Be available at no cost.

If your story meets these criteria, please email the description (and the link to additional information) to us and we will review it for addition to our site.
